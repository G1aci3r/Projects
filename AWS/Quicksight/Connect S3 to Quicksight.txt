Quick Guide to linked S3 Bucket to Quicksight

Amazon S3
1.) Create a new bucket in Amaxon S3 or use existing bucket.
      - If creating a new bucket, keep the default setting and create a bucket name.
2.) Open bucket and upload 'Amazon-Bestseller-Dataset.csv'.
3.) Open 'manifest.json' and on line 5 change "bucket-name" to your S3 bucket name.
4.) Upload edited 'manifest.json' into your bucket.

Amazon Quicksight
1.) Open Quicksight (create or login into an existing account)
2.) Click on Dataset on the left hand side and select S3.
3.) Navigate back to S3 bucket and click on your manifest.json. Click 'Copy S3 URI'.  
4.) Head back to Quicksight and paste URL under 'Upload a manifest file'.
5.) Name data source
6.) Then click visualize



Done
